GUIDE

Instructions that the students has to follow in college campus

• Students should only wear a formal dress (Formal shirts and Pants) so as to bring out their professional stature.
• During placement and career related events and other formal functions like conferences, boys should necessarily tuck in their shirts and wear formal shoes.
• Girls should follow the dress code prescribed by the Training and Placement Cell.
• Students coming in a casual manner will not be allowed to take part in any of the events.
• Pants with dress with tight fittings and above the knee length will not be allowed.
• Shirts should be not in gaudy colors and should not be of semi formal or casual category.
• Bathroom slippers and other casual footwear will not be allowed. Dress and Slippers with any wordings / writings and pictures will not be allowed.
• Students should not sport mushroom cut kind of fancy hair styles, fancy beards and fancy moustaches. Hair coloring is not allowed. Boys should not wear Ear studs for fancy reasons unless the religious reasons call for wearing the same.
• Girls should follow the dress code that fully conforms with the Professional Indian style / attire
• Students should not venture into isolated places which are unassigned to them and where there is no academic or other activities happening or pertaining to their stay in the campus or for sports.
• Students should always have / wear their ID cards with them for proving their identity whenever requested by the authorities.
• Polite and courteous behaviour with friends and teachers and professional behaviour as audience during meetings / classrooms (by being attentive to the speaker / not using mobile phones / not involving in talking etc.) is expected.
• Students are advised to discuss with their parents about the importance of these instructions and get suitable attire for their wardrobe in conformance with the above instructions.
